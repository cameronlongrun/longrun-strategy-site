About Us

An independent partner across the investment lifecycle.

What We Do

Focused advisory work across the investment lifecycle.

01

Commercial Due Diligence

We pressure-test the commercial case behind an investment: market size and growth, competitive dynamics, customer concentration, and pricing power. Investors and acquirers use our findings to underwrite with confidence and negotiate from a position of knowledge.

02

Growth & Market Strategy

For corporate leaders, boards, and portfolio companies alike, we build growth strategy grounded in evidence and sized to the opportunity. Market segmentation, whitespace identification, and go to market plans that a board can approve and a team can execute.

03

Portfolio Value Creation

Between entry and exit is where value gets made or lost. We work alongside management on the specific, prioritized initiatives that move EBITDA: pricing, channel strategy, category expansion, and operational focus.

04

Competitive & Landscape Assessment

A clear view of where a category is headed and who is positioned to win it. We map competitors, private label and emerging entrants, channel shifts, and category trends, so you are always ready for a competitor's next move.

05

Exit Readiness & Vendor Due Diligence

We help sponsors and management teams tell the growth story to the next buyer, backed by the same rigor we would want to see if we were sitting on the other side of the table.

How We Work

A small firm, held to a high standard.

01

Scope tightly

Every engagement starts with a clear set of questions built around your decision. We agree on what the work needs to prove before a single slide gets built.

02

Go to the source

We rely on primary research, industry data, and direct conversations with customers, competitors, and channel partners, going well beyond desk research and press releases.

03

Write it plainly

Findings are delivered in clear, direct language, in a deck built for the investment committee and the decision at hand.

04

Stay accountable

We remain available after the deck is delivered. A thesis is only as good as its execution, and we want to see it through.
