About Us

An independent partner across the investment lifecycle.

Longrun Strategy is an independent advisory firm built to help private equity investors make better decisions, before a deal closes and long after it does. Our team pairs the analytical rigor of top-tier strategy consulting with real operating experience building and scaling companies from the inside.

That blend matters. We have run the diligence, and we have lived with the decisions it informs, so our work stays grounded in what actually moves a business.

We specialize in commercial and vendor due diligence, market sizing, growth strategy, and value creation, with particular depth in international go-to-market and geographic expansion, M&A advisory, and helping leadership teams make high-stakes decisions with clarity and confidence.

The name says how we think. Good investing is a marathon, not a sprint, and the best answer is the one that still holds years after the deal closes: in the data room, in the committee meeting, and in the market.

What We Do

Focused advisory work across the investment lifecycle.

01

Commercial Due Diligence

We pressure-test the commercial case behind an investment: market size and growth, competitive dynamics, customer concentration, and pricing power. Sponsors use our findings to underwrite with confidence and negotiate from a position of knowledge.

02

Growth & Market Strategy

For portfolio companies and management teams, we build growth strategy grounded in evidence and sized to the opportunity. Market segmentation, whitespace identification, and go to market plans that a board can approve and a team can execute.

03

Portfolio Value Creation

Between entry and exit is where value gets made or lost. We work alongside management on the specific, prioritized initiatives that move EBITDA: pricing, channel strategy, category expansion, and operational focus.

04

Competitive & Landscape Assessment

A clear view of where a category is headed and who is positioned to win it. We map competitors, private label and emerging entrants, channel shifts, and category trends, so you are never surprised by a competitor's next move.

05

Exit Readiness & Vendor Due Diligence

We help sponsors and management teams tell the growth story to the next buyer, backed by the same rigor we would want to see if we were sitting on the other side of the table.

How We Work

A small firm, held to a high standard.

01

Scope tightly

Every engagement starts with a clear set of questions built around your decision. We agree on what the work needs to prove before a single slide gets built.

02

Go to the source

04

Stay accountable

We remain available after the deck is delivered. A thesis is only as good as its execution, and we want to see it through.
